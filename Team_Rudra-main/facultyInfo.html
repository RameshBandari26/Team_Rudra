Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao

Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao (Ph.D., IIT Madras) is an accomplished academician, research innovator, and strategic institutional leader, currently serving as Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering at RGUKT Basar. A proud alumnus of IIT Madras, he holds a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in polymer composites, fracture and durability mechanics, non-destructive testing (NDT), and eco-sustainable engineering solutions.

With over 11 years of academic, research, and administrative experience, Dr. Rao has consistently driven excellence across diverse verticals — be it in teaching, mentoring, or institutional development. His contributions have been pivotal in shaping a research-driven and innovation-centric ecosystem at RGUKT Basar.

He was one of the earliest champions of entrepreneurship and applied research on campus, having launched student innovation cells and incubation initiatives as early as 2016. Under his mentorship, RGUKT Basar celebrated its first national triumph in the Smart India Hackathon (Hardware Edition), and since then, he has guided students to file patents, develop prototypes, and win accolades at national platforms.

Research & Innovation

A strong proponent of interdisciplinary collaboration, Dr. Rao led a ₹12 lakh DSIR-funded research project on sustainable construction materials, and has also supervised research on lightweight composites at NIT Warangal. He has published extensively in internationally reputed journals such as Construction & Building Materials and Materials Today: Proceedings, contributing significantly to the fields of structural durability and green technologies.

Vision & Impact

Dr. Rao has facilitated 25+ MoUs with global institutions and industry giants like Microsoft, SAP, and Wells Fargo — connecting students to internships, certifications, and international research. Nearly 30 students have gained global exposure under his mentorship. His work bridges academia and industry, ensuring RGUKT students are globally competitive and socially responsible.

“More than a mentor — he is a catalyst of transformation, igniting minds with knowledge, grounding them with discipline, and inspiring them with vision.”
