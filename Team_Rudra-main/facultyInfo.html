Faculty Mentor – Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao (Ph.D., IIT Madras)

Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao, Associate Professor at RGUKT Basar and mentor of Team Rudra, is much more than a faculty advisor — he’s the backbone of our vision and execution. With a Ph.D. from IIT Madras and a Management Essentials certification from IIM Shillong, he combines deep technical expertise with strategic foresight.

Research & Innovation

With research interests in polymer composites, fracture mechanics, and sustainable engineering, Dr. Rao consistently pushes the boundaries of innovation. Under his mentorship, Team Rudra thrives — from design validations and safety checks to real-time problem solving, his involvement ensures we stay grounded in engineering excellence while aiming high.

Key Achievements

  • Mentored RGUKT’s first Smart India Hackathon national-winning team (2019)
  • Guided two patent filings and mentored award-winning student startups like xpertree.com and pubvision.io
  • Led a ₹12 Lakh DSIR-sponsored project and contributed to the world’s first air-conditioned helmet
  • Facilitated 25+ international MoUs for global research exposure
  • Recipient of the Telangana State Best Teacher Award (2024)

Leadership & Service

  • Dean (R&D), Former Controller of Examinations, Advisor (Exams)
  • Faculty Representative – Training & Placement
  • Established labs, secured AICTE approvals, streamlined UG admissions
  • Coordinated certifications (Microsoft, SAP, CSWA), FDPs, CNC training, IEI student chapters

Vision & Impact

Whether mentoring innovation, reforming systems, or inspiring students — Dr. Rao stands as a symbol of what visionary academia can achieve. His belief in student potential and commitment to excellence continues to shape not just our project, but the future of engineering at RGUKT.

“A mentor, leader, and visionary shaping future engineers through unwavering dedication and innovation.”
