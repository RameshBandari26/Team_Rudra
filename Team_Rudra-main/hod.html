Mr. Abbadi Charan Reddy

Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering – RGUKT Basar

Mr. Abbadi Charan Reddy is a dedicated academician and researcher with a strong background in mechanical engineering. A postgraduate from NIT Warangal, his expertise spans 3D Printing, Composite Materials, and Tribology.

Academic & Administrative Roles

Since joining RGUKT Basar in 2016, he has played an active role in both academics and administration — mentoring students, coordinating technical events, and contributing to campus discipline and examination processes.

Research Contributions

His research work has been recognized at international conferences, with notable publications including:

  • Friction stir processed composites
  • Performance analysis of polymer-metal joints

Campus Engagement

Beyond the classroom, he has also served as:

  • Hostel Warden
  • Faculty Coordinator
  • Library In-charge

This reflects his commitment to holistic student development and leadership on campus.

“A mentor who brings innovation, encouragement, and practical insights into every student journey.”
