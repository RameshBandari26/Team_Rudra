About Us

We are Team Rudra, the first-ever Formula Student team from RGUKT Basar, built by 27 passionate and self-driven mechanical engineering students under the guidance of Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao. With a deep focus on innovation, teamwork, and technical excellence, we're designing and building a high-performance formula-style race car to proudly represent our institute at FFS India 2025. Driven by curiosity. Defined by commitment.
