Prof. Enelli Murali Darshan

Prof. Murali Darshan is a visionary leader and one of the key pillars in RGUKT Basar’s academic and innovation ecosystem. As Officer on Special Duty (OSD), he steers high-impact programs and ensures seamless coordination across departments — combining administrative excellence with strategic foresight.

Initiatives and Leadership

He played a prominent role in launching the RGUKT SDG Summit in March 2025, working alongside the Vice Chancellor to promote:

  • Sustainability
  • Research Collaboration
  • Community Outreach in Rural Engineering

Support for Student Innovation

He actively supports student-led innovation platforms, including the IEEE Student Branch. Under his guidance, students receive:

  • Mentorship from faculty and industry
  • Access to resources and labs
  • Opportunities for national-level engagement

Vision and Impact

Prof. Darshan’s work represents a broader aim: to connect RGUKT Basar's rural talent with global sustainability challenges, fostering world-class exposure through events, summits, and community-driven projects.

“An enabling force behind our academic and innovation transformation — he translates vision into action.”
