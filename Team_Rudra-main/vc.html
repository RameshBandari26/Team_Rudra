Prof. A. Govardhan

Prof. A. Govardhan, Vice Chancellor of RGUKT Basar, is a distinguished academic and administrator with a B.E., M.Tech, Ph.D. (CSE), and a Postgraduate Diploma in Leadership from EMERITUS (Singapore). He brings a wealth of expertise in teaching, research, and institutional leadership, holding a Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from JNTU Hyderabad.

Teaching and Academic Contributions

Prof. Govardhan has taught over 20 undergraduate and postgraduate subjects including:

  • Advanced Computing
  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Software Engineering

Professional Affiliations

He is a Fellow or Member of several prestigious organizations, such as:

  • CSI (Computer Society of India)
  • IEI (Institution of Engineers India)
  • IETE, IEEE, ACM, ISCA, and AAAS

Leadership at RGUKT Basar

As Vice Chancellor, Prof. Govardhan has been instrumental in transforming RGUKT Basar by:

  • Expanding ICT-enabled learning across all disciplines
  • Strengthening research initiatives and industry partnerships
  • Introducing institutional credit transfers for SWAYAM–NPTEL courses

Under his leadership, RGUKT Basar ranked among India’s top 2% local NPTEL chapters.

Vision for Innovation

Prof. Govardhan actively promotes innovation in rural engineering by empowering students to solve real-world problems and emerge as future-ready tech leaders.

“A transformative leader who empowers rural talent through vision, action, and academic excellence.”
