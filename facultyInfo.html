Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao P

Dr. V. Chandra Sekhara Rao P is an esteemed academician with over 11 years of experience in mechanical engineering. He earned his Ph.D. from IIT Madras, focusing on the "Mechanical, Fracture, and Durability Characteristics of Polymer Composites with Variations in Micro Fillers, Macro Fillers, and Fibers." Since joining RGUKT in 2016, he has held significant roles including Assistant Professor and Former Controller of Examinations.

Research and Projects

Dr. Rao has led impactful research, notably a ₹12 lakh DSIR-sponsored project developing cost-effective, high-strength construction materials using recycled waste plastic.

Publications and Conferences

He has published work in leading journals such as Construction and Building Materials and Materials Today: Proceedings, and has presented at several national and international conferences.

Academic and Administrative Roles

At RGUKT, Dr. Rao has served as:

  • Controller of Examinations
  • Interim Head of the Mechanical Engineering Department
  • Coordinator, Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell

He has mentored student teams who achieved national recognition in events like the Smart India Hackathon.

Research Interests

  • High Strength High Performance Structural Materials
  • Fracture Mechanics
  • Polymer Matrix Composites
  • Non-Destructive Testing
  • Renewable Energy Sources
